If you are at a low risk of having Diabetes.Follow this steps to keep it in control

Healthy Diet

Plan well balanced , nutritional meals

Regular Exercise

Choose an exercise that you enjoy ,such as walking,swimming,etc.

Diabetes medication

Your doctor may prescribe diabetes medications that help lower insulin levels.

Say no to alcohol

Alcohol can make some of the complications of diabetes worse.

Weight Loss

Weight loss results in better control of blood sugar levels.

Blood sugar monitoring

Need to check it once in a day and before or after exercise
