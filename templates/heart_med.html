If you are at a low risk of having Heart Disease.Follow this steps to keep it in control

Keep cholestrol under control

High cholestrol can clog your arteries and raise your risk of heart disease

Regular Exercise

Choose an exercise that you enjoy ,such as walking,swimming,etc.

Control your Blood pressure

High blood pressure is one of the biggest risk factors for heart disease.

Limit alcohol intake

Too much alcohol can raise your blood pressure and adds extra calories to your diet

Manage Stress

Stress can affect the heart in all sort of ways ,including raising blood pressure and even triggering heart attack.

Manage Diabetes

High blood sugar can damage your blood vessels .Get screened for diabets regularly.
